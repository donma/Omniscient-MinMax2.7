VORTEX RS SETS NEW NÜRBURGRING RECORD

The VORTEX RS has completed the legendary Nürburgring Nordschleife in an astonishing 6:42.3, setting a new benchmark for production sports cars in its class. This remarkable achievement showcases the relentless pursuit of performance that defines every VORTEX vehicle.

The record run was conducted under strict production car regulations, meaning the vehicle tested was identical to those available to customers. Our engineering team spent countless hours optimizing every aspect of the RS to achieve this result.

"This record demonstrates what our team is capable of achieving," said our Chief Performance Officer. "The RS represents the pinnacle of our sports car engineering, and this time proves that it's ready to compete with the world's best."
